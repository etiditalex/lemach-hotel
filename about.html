About Lemach

Tucked within Kenya’s north coast, Lemach Hotel pairs bright, restful rooms with the flavors of coastal cuisine. Guests start days with tropical breakfasts, unwind by the pool or garden nooks, and explore the nearby creek, beaches, and historic sites.

Positioning

Mid‑upscale comfort with local character.

USPs

Fresh coastal menu, breezy rooms, warm service, easy access to beach & town.

Audience

Couples • Families • Business travelers • Small groups

Facilities & Services

Leisure

Outdoor pool, sun loungers, garden nooks, curated experiences (dhow cruise, reef snorkel, forest walk).

Business

Meeting room up to 20, projector/TV, printing on request, fast Wi‑Fi.

Guest Services

24/7 reception, concierge, laundry, transfers, car hire assistance.

Operations
  • Check‑in/out: 14:00 / 10:00
  • Front Desk: 24 hrs
  • Payments: M‑Pesa, cards, bank transfer
  • Accessibility: Ground‑floor rooms; ramp access
  • Languages: English, Swahili
Policies
  • Cancellation: Free until 3 days before; later = 1 night charge
  • No‑show: 100% of first night
  • Children: All ages welcome; cribs on request
  • Pets: Small pets by prior request
  • Smoking: Designated outdoor areas only
Sustainability

Linen reuse program; single‑use plastic reduction; local sourcing.

Location & Access

Kilifi Creek, pristine beaches, Gede Ruins, Arabuko‑Sokoke Forest, dhow trips, kitesurfing, kaya forests. Good road access from Mombasa and Malindi; private transfers can be arranged.
