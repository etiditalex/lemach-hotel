Swahili spice notes: coconut, tamarind & cardamom

By The Lemach Kitchen • 5 min read

Coastal Kenyan cuisine layers gentle heat with aromatic spices. At Lemach, we lean on fresh coconut milk, tart tamarind, and floral cardamom to bring balance to seafood and vegetables.

  • Coconut adds body and silkiness to curries without heaviness.
  • Tamarind brightens grilled octopus and prawns with tangy depth.
  • Cardamom perfumes rice and desserts like mango & lime cheesecake.

Ask for our market specials—the team sources from local fishermen and farmers daily.
